About Us

Welcome to EPIGNATHUS, a platform dedicated to assisting surgical residents navigate their rotations and excel in membership examinations, such as those offered by the GCPS or WACS. Our mission is to equip you with the knowledge, resources, and guidance needed to succeed.

The comprehensive resources, including recommended textbooks, past questions, and quizzes, are tailored to enhance your learning experience and help you excel in your surgical journey.
