Sample Quiz

1. What is the most common cause of acute appendicitis?

Correct answer: Fecalith

2. Which of the following is the best initial treatment for intussusception in a child?

Correct answer: Air enema

3. What is the first-line treatment for a small pneumothorax?

Correct answer: Observation

4. What is the most common cause of large bowel obstruction in adults?

Correct answer: Colon cancer

5. What is the initial treatment for an acute anal fissure?

Correct answer: High-fiber diet and sitz baths

6. Which imaging modality is most sensitive for detecting cholelithiasis?

Correct answer: Ultrasound

7. What is the most common type of hernia in adults?

Correct answer: Inguinal hernia

8. Which of the following is a complication of peptic ulcer disease?

Correct answer: Perforation

9. What is the primary indication for a Whipple procedure?

Correct answer: Pancreatic cancer

10. Which of the following is the best initial imaging study for suspected bowel obstruction?

Correct answer: X-ray
